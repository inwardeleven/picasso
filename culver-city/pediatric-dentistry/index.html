Pediatric Dentistry

West Los Angeles Dental Care For Kids

We know choosing a pediatric dentist for your child is an important medical decision for your family. You want your kid's dentist to understand your child's needs and to deliver the right treatment to your child. When you take your child to Picasso Smiles, your little one will receive personalized, high-quality dental care from our Culver City pediatric dentists, Drs. Tamir Shams and Nima Shayesteh. Additionally, our friendly dentists and experienced team make every effort to make children feel comfortable and less anxious about their treatment.


Your Child's Pediatric Dental Visit At Picasso Smiles

We pride ourselves in the special care we provide for your little one the moment he or she walks into our Culver City dental practice. Our pediatric dentists strongly believe that oral health begins during the early childhood years. When your child visits Picasso Smiles, our leading pediatric dentists will determine the proper dental care for your kid. For instance, to prevent tooth decay, we may do the following for added protection:

  • Fluoride Treatment
  • Dental Sealants
  • Dental Sedation (For Extensive Cases)

Furthermore, your child's visit with our Los Angeles dentists will also consist of education for you and your little one about the best oral hygiene methods for healthy dental habits.


Contact Your Local Culver City Pediatric Dentists Today!

Thank you for choosing Picasso Smiles as your primary dental practice for your child. Our practice also offers comprehensive dental care, including cosmetic dentistry and dental implants.
