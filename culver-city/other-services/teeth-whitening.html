Teeth Whitening in West Los Angeles

At Picasso Smiles Dental, we make it our goal to give each and every one of our patients in or around West Los Angeles and Culver City, CA the best possible smile. In order to accomplish this, we're proud to offer a wide variety of cosmetic dentistry treatments to fix any imperfections with your teeth or your smile. The safest and most requested cosmetic dentistry procedure that we provide at our Culver City office is teeth whitening. If you're looking for a brighter and whiter smile, you've come to the right place!


Zoom! Laser Teeth Whitening

Zoom! Whitening is used to lighten tooth enamel and dentin through a bleaching process. This procedure will usually take about one hour. While the laser light's warmth should alleviate any potential discomfort, you may feel a little sensitivity. If you often have sensitive gums, please ask the dentist for anti-sensitivity toothpaste prior to your teeth whitening treatment.


Our Teeth Whitening Procedure

To whiten your teeth, Zoom! Whitening works by following these steps:

  • First, hydrogen peroxide (the bleaching agent) is activated by the laser.
  • The light breaks down the hydrogen peroxide.
  • Oxygen will then enter your enamel and dentin.
  • The oxygen will bleach any colored substances while leaving the rest of the tooth unchanged.

To keep your teeth white, it's important for you to follow up your treatment with proper oral care. This includes brushing twice a day, flossing daily, and applying occasional touch-ups with Zoom! Weekender or Nite White gel.
