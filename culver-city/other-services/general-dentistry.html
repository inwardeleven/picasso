Our General Dentistry Treatments

If you're looking for a family dentist in the Culver City area, Picasso Smiles Dental is here for you! In order to help you maintain optimal dental health, we provide these general dentistry treatments.


Dental Exams

For the recommended annual oral exam, our dentists have general dentistry expertise to conduct these check up to monitor for any dental issues, including gum disease, tooth decay, and the condition of any restorations. Our Culver City office utilizes the latest technology, including digital x-rays, to provide the most comprehensive dental exams for the best care.


Teeth Cleanings

Routine teeth cleanings are crucial to optimal oral health. Teeth cleanings should be scheduled every six months to remove plaque from your teeth that regular daily brushing can't reach.


Fillings

In the case of a damaged tooth, our Culver City dentists place fillings to restore the tooth. At our Culver City dental office, we use only white fillings that are high quality and aesthetically pleasing.
