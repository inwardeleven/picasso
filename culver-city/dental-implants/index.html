Comprehensive Dental Implant Treatment in Culver City

For all those in or around West Los Angeles and Culver City, CA who have damaged or missing teeth, we offer dental implant surgery that can give you a smile you once thought you could only dream of. Our dental implants can replace your missing teeth and no one will even realize that they’re not your natural teeth!


What Are Dental Implants?

A dental implant is essentially a prosthesis that’s used in order to replace a missing tooth. It’s a small titanium post that’s inserted into the jawbone to replace the roots of a missing tooth. It will take time for an implant to fuse with the jawbone, so during this time, a temporary crown will be placed on the dental implant. After the implant properly fuses with the jawbone, the temporary crown will be removed and replaced with a permanent and natural-looking crown.


The Advantages of Dental Implants

There are many different benefits for choosing a dental implant, including:


  • Dental implants look, feel, and work the exact same as natural teeth.
  • Dental implants will improve your chewing function and allow you to eat anything you want.
  • They can prevent a sunken facial appearance that is typically caused by tooth loss.
  • Implants won't grind against nearby teeth in the way that dental bridges do.
  • With proper care and good hygiene, a dental implant can last a lifetime.


About Our Culver City, CA Dental Implant Process

Some key points about our dental implant procedure are:


  • You'll be under local anesthesia so you shouldn’t feel any pain or discomfort.
  • The healing period can last up to six months.
  • The entire dental implant process can take up to nine months.
  • Dental implants have about a 90 percent success rate, with the vast majority of the failures being due to a patient not maintaining good oral hygiene.
  • We implement a Soft Tissue Management program for all patients that undergo a dental implant procedure.

Ideal candidates for dental implants must have enough bone structure, don't smoke or drink alcohol, and do not have diabetic or healing problems.
