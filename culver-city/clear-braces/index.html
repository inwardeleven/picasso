Our Culver City Clear Braces

Clear braces, also known as Invisalign or ClearCorrect, is a newer orthodontic treatment option that's perfect for those who are looking to straighten their smile without the usage of noticeable wires and brackets. Clear braces work by utilizing a series of custom-made, removable aligners made of plastic that are also virtually invisible. Others won't even be able to tell that you're undergoing orthodontic treatment.


The Advantages of Clear Braces

Some of the advantages of choosing clear braces include:

  • A discrete form of orthodontic treatment.
  • Aligners can be removed for eating and easy brushing.
  • The plastic aligners are more comfortable than metal wires and brackets that can irritate the mouth and gums.
  • They can whiten teeth simultaneously.
  • The last aligner can also be used as a retainer.

Combining Clear Braces and Veneers

In certain cases, it's even more beneficial to combine clear braces with veneers to give yourself a more dramatically improved smile. This combination of the two treatments will take advantage of the minimally invasive properties of clear braces and the precision of veneers.
