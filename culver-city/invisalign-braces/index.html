Los Angeles Dentistry & Orthodontic Care

Picasso Smiles Dental offers a wide variety of dental services including cosmetic dentistry, implant dentistry, teeth whitening, veneers, pediatric dentistry and more. What you may not know is that our office is not limited to offering just dental services. In fact, Picasso Smiles Dental offers orthodontic treatments such as our Invisalign or ClearCorrect clear aligners as a great alternative to traditional metal braces.


What is Invisalign | ClearCorrect?

Invisalign or ClearCorrect are great treatment options for those looking to improve their smiles without the look or feel of metal wires and brackets. These clear braces options work by using a series of custom-made clear aligners that are virtually undetectable to the naked eye. At Picasso Smiles Dental, we will create a customized treatment plan during our initial consultation to determine what is best for you.


The advantages of Clear Braces

  • Clear, custom made aligners
  • Aligners can be removed for eating, drinking & brushing/ flossing
  • No metal wires or brackets that irritate the gums
  • Can whiten teeth simultaneously
  • The last tray can be used as your retainer

Combining Invisalign | ClearCorrect & Veneers

In some cases, it is very beneficial to use both Invisalign | ClearCorrect and porcelain veneers for a more dramatic look. The hybrid of the two treatments takes advantage of the minimally invasive properties of Invisalign | ClearCorrect with the precision and exactness of veneers.
