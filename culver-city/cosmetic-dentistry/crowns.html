Lava and Porcelain Crowns

Culver City Cosmetic Dentistry

The days of metal crowns are over! The doctors at Picasso Smiles Dental are happy to offer porcelain and lava crowns that are discrete and effective. Crown Treatments are used to repair broken, cracked or filled teeth for moderate to severe dental decay while restoring the aesthetics of your smile. The crowns are made of 100% all-porcelain or all-Zirconia, which tends to be stronger and less fragile and much more esthetic. That means you don't have to worry about unsightly metal margins disfiguring your smile.


Benefits of Choosing Crowns

Crowns are great treatments because the size, shape, texture, and color can be customized for each patient so that it matches your other teeth identically and looks natural. Some advantages include:


  • Custom made crowns for efficient matching
  • RRestoring the aesthetics and function of your broken, chipped or cracked teeth
  • Most teeth can be restored in two dental visits

BruxZir Crowns

At Picasso Smiles Dental, we use BruxZir crowns that truly deliver in strength and aesthetics. These conventional crowns are made of a metal substructure with porcelain over it. They are created with 100% zirconia which means much higher strength, no chipping, discoloration, and they appear to be much more natural looking than traditional crowns. These include no additional fees unlike other dental offices. Contact Dr. Nima or Dr. Shams for a consultation today.
