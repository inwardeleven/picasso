Veneers

What is there to do when you simply have a suboptimal tooth that may not be in that bad of shape? In these circumstances, we at Picasso Smiles Dental offer a variety of cosmetic dentistry treatments in order to fix or even just improve a smile. One such treatment that we offer to all those in and around West Los Angeles and Culver City, CA is the placement of veneers.


What Are Veneers?

A dental veneer is a thin layer of porcelain material that's placed over a tooth. A veneer is like an alternative to a crown. A veneer can be used to protect and enhance the appearance of your teeth.


What Is The Process?

It's SIMPLE! Schedule a consultation with one of our certified Culver City dentists. A specific plan of action will be catered to your needs by you and the doctor. Come in twice for the service and go home with that elegant smile you've been waiting for.


The Advantages of Veneers

Veneers provide a variety of benefits to patients. Some of the reasons why you may want to choose a veneer include, but aren't limited to, the following:


  • Veneers can restore function and aesthetics to broken or cracked teeth.
  • They provide a natural appearance.
  • They can be used to make darker or discolored teeth look whiter.
  • The process of getting a veneer doesn't require the extensive shaping and dental work that crowns do.
  • Veneers can lengthen smaller or misshapen teeth.
  • They can also fill in gaps between teeth.
