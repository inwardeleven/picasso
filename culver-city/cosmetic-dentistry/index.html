Smile with Confidence!

It's unfortunate, but there are many people out there who are too afraid to laugh or even smile. And when they do, they typically hold a hand up to cover their laugh or smile. The reason for this is because they don’t feel confident in their smile and they don’t want people to see it. If you feel like this may be you, a complete smile makeover may be the right choice. We offer a wide variety of cosmetic dentistry treatments that are able to be customized based on your specific needs. Our cosmetic dental treatments are capable of:


  • Changing the shape, size, and alignment of your teeth.
  • Correcting or even improving your bite.
  • Filling in any gaps or unattractive spaces in between your teeth.
  • Whitening the color of your teeth.
  • Repairing your chipped, cracked, decayed, or broken teeth.
  • Replacing your missing teeth.
  • Replacing any old and unattractive dental treatments.

With just a few visits to our Culver City office, our team will be able to transform your smile – and all in time for a vacation, job interview, or any other special event. With the help of our sedation dentistry, we can even often complete a smile makeover in just one visit!
