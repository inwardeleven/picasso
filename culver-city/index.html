Welcome to Picasso Smiles Dental

Your Culver City Dentist

Picasso Smiles Dental is your premier choice in and around Culver City, CA when you’re looking for a dentist who can do it all. From high-quality cosmetic dentistry and dental implants to teeth whitening and clear braces, we aim to be your dentist of choice when it comes to all your dental needs. With our cutting-edge technology and top-of-the-line equipment, we’re capable of delivering efficient and effective dental treatments as fast as possible and with as little discomfort as possible. Not only do we focus on technology, but we also place an emphasis on patient care and comfort. We pride ourselves on our relaxing and family-friendly office environment that’s perfect for patients of all ages.


Our Culver City dentists, Dr. Tamir Shams and Dr. Nima Shayesteh, are highly-trained and experienced professionals who continuously further their education in order to keep up with the latest technological advances in the field of dentistry. Dr. Shams and Dr. Shayesteh have spent years servicing the community of Culver City, CA and are happy to go above and beyond to give you the dental care that you deserve.
