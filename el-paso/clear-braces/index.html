El Paso Invisalign & Clear Correct

With new and advanced technology, there are now alternatives to traditional braces that make orthodontic treatment more comfortable and discreet. As your trusted choice for all your dentistry needs, we at Picasso Smiles Dental are proud to offer clear braces to patients in and around El Paso.


What Are Clear Braces?

Clear braces are an alternative orthodontic treatment to traditional braces. Picasso Smiles Dental offers the Invisalign and ClearCorrect systems to our patients. Invisalign and ClearCorrect work through the usage of a series of custom-made aligners that will gradually shift teeth over time that will allow a patient to obtain a straight and healthy smile without the need for wires and brackets. At Picasso Smiles Dental, we'll create a customized treatment plan for you during our initial consultation to determine what's best for you.


What Are the Advantages of Invisalign & ClearCorrect?

Some of the advantages of choosing Invisalign or ClearCorrect include:

  • The aligners you wear are virtually invisible.
  • The aligners can be removed for when you eat and drink and when you brush and floss your teeth.
  • Unlike traditional braces, you can eat whatever you want when undergoing this treatment.
  • Invisalign and ClearCorrect are more comfortable; as there are no metal wires or brackets that can irritate your mouth or gums involved in treatment.
  • You can simultaneously whiten your teeth while undergoing treatment.
  • The last aligner can be used as your retainer.

Combining Invisalign Or ClearCorrect with Veneers

For a more dramatic change in your smile, you can sometimes combine veneers with Invisalign. This hybrid of the two treatments will improve your teeth with the precision and exactness of veneers along with the minimal invasive properties of Invisalign.
