Bringing Healthy Smiles To The Westside & Eastside

Thank you for choosing Drs. Tamir Shams and Nima Shayesteh at Picasso Smiles Dental as your local El Paso dentists. We are excited to see you and want you to arrive to your appointment on time and safely. Below you will find information for each of our El Paso office locations, as well as special points of interest to make sure you know exactly where to find us.


Are you on your way to our Westside office?


Our office is located in the Shadow Mountain Shopping Center and our lovely neighbors are Eloise Restaurant, Bar, & Coffee Shop and Cuts R Us.



We are conveniently located 3.5 miles south to interstate 10 and just .3 miles south of TX-20. If you are coming from the south and find yourself browsing the Burlington Coat Factory selection, you have gone a bit too far. If you are coming from the north and drive by the post office, back track just a little bit to find Picasso Smiles.
