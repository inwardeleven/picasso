Insurance & Financing

Affordable Treatment from Your El Paso Dentists

Thank you for choosing Picasso Smiles Dental as your premier dental practice. Where Dr. Tamir Shams' and Dr. Nima Shayesteh's motto is that every patient deserves a dazzling smile. To comfortably and affordably achieve this smile we are in-network providers for most major dental insurances and easy financing is also available with little or no interest payment plans. If you have any questions regarding your dental insurance carrier, do not hesitate to contact our office and a team member will be happy to assist you.

No insurance? No problem, a team member will also help you with available financing and easy affordable payment plans.
