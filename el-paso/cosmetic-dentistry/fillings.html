White Fillings

Your Premier El Paso Cosmetic Dentistry

Picasso Smiles provides those living in the El Paso, TX area with high quality composite resins, or white fillings, that will have you smiling with confidence in no time.  We feel that white fillings not only have a beneficial health factor, but they allow each and every one of our patients’ to smile without feeling insecure.


Cosmetic Benefits of White Fillings

Probably the best thing about white fillings is that no one will notice you have them! White fillings are practically invisible, especially when compared to the metal alternative. These white fillings are easily placed; in fact, you can have your white fillings placed in just one visit.


Health Benefits of White Fillings

Although Dr. Shams and Dr. Shayesteh wish to provide you with a beautiful smile, your health is what is most important to them. With this in mind, the doctors and staff at Picasso Smiles have decided not to provide their patients with metal fillings. Metal fillings contain a significant amount of mercury, which can be harmful to your kidneys and nervous system.

 

Composite fillings will be able to protect your teeth from mild tooth decay just as well as metal ones without the potential health side effects and unattractiveness.
