Veneers

We’ve made it our goal at Picasso Smiles Dental to give each and every one of our patients a smile that they can be proud of. As your El Paso dentists, we’re here to provide the cosmetic dentistry treatments needed to help transform your smile into something that’s absolutely breathtaking. No matter if you have severely decayed or even missing teeth, we have the dental treatments that will restore your smile. If you happen to be looking for a non-invasive way to improve an imperfection in your smile, we’d like to recommend our dental veneers.


What Are Veneers?

Dental veneers are thin layers of a porcelain material that are placed over a tooth. Veneers can be used to both protect your teeth and improve the appearance of them. Veneers work in a similar manner to crowns, only the process for receiving one is much quicker and more convenient.


Our Veneer Process

Getting a veneer at Picasso Smiles Dental is simple. After you’ve scheduled a consultation with one of our dentists, we’ll create a customized plan that’s catered to your needs. You’ll just have to come in to visit us twice for our service and afterward, you’ll be walking away with a beautiful, new smile.


Advantages of Our El Paso Veneers

Some of the reasons why you should consider getting a veneer include the following:


  • Veneers can repair chipped, broken, discolored, or misshapen teeth.
  • Veneers will restore both the function and aesthetic appeal to damaged teeth.
  • They’ll give you a natural appearance that’s indistinguishable from your other teeth.
  • The process of getting a veneer is much simpler than getting a crown.
  • Veneers can also fix minor orthodontic problems like filling in gaps between teeth.
