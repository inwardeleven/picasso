Smile With Pride

If the idea of smiling terrifies you because of various dental concerns, a complete smile makeover may be right for you. Picasso Smiles cosmetic dentistry in El Paso combines treatments and customizes them based on your specific needs. Our cosmetic dental treatments can:


  • Change the size, shape, and alignment of your teeth
  • Fill in unattractive spaces in between your teeth
  • Improve or correct your bite
  • Lighten or brighten the color of your teeth
  • Repair your broken, cracked, chipped, or decayed teeth
  • Replace your missing teeth
  • Replace your old, unattractive dental treatments

After just a few visits, our team can transform your smile in time for that special event, vacation, or all-important job interview. And with sedation, we can complete a smile makeover in one visit!
