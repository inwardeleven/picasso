CBCT/Dental CT Scan

CBCT provides rapid volumetric images of jaws and teeth that can be visualized accurately with excellent resolution, and can be viewed from any angle. This 3-D imaging modality is emerging as the "standard of care" for a number of diagnostic assessments of the bony components of the face and for the more precise planning and placement of dental implants, extraction of wisdom teeth, and root canal treatments. At Picasso Smiles, it is the standard of care. In implant treatment, appropriate site or size can be chosen before placement. Remarkably, our Picasso Smiles' surgeons plan their implant surgery on the computer and can fabricate a Customized Surgical Guide to direct the exact placement of the dental implant(s) with great precision. Our Envivo5 software from Anatomage, also allows us to even perform "virtual" implant surgery before the actual surgical procedure takes place.
