Do I Need Oral Surgery?

There may be many reasons why a patient may require oral surgery. Whatever the reason may be, you can be sure that you will receive the best care in the Northeast El Paso area. The reasons you may need oral surgery are listed below:


  • Wisdom teeth: You may need wisdom teeth removed so that further damage to other teeth can be halted.
  • Protruding teeth: If a tooth or teeth are protruding, you may need oral surgery to have the issue taken care of. Protruding teeth risk being broken and may damage other healthy teeth.
  • Impacted teeth: Impacted teeth can cause an undesirable shift in other teeth.

In many instances, oral surgery may not be necessary. For those rare occasions that action needs to be taken however, our doctors are here to guide you along the way.
