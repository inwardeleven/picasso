Teeth Whitening

According to the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry, teeth whitening is the most requested cosmetic dentistry procedure there is. This should come as no surprise – having whiter and brighter teeth will make your smile look much more attractive and possibly give you some added confidence as well. As your El Paso dentistry providers, we at Picasso Smiles Dental are proud to offer a safe and efficient teeth whitening procedure - Zoom! Whitening.

Our Zoom! Teeth Whitening Treatment


Zoom! Whitening will lighten your tooth enamel and dentin through a bleaching process. This 45-minute procedure works by following these steps:

  • First, we’ll apply a whitening gel to your teeth.
  • After the gel is applied, we’ll use our special laser to activate the hydrogen peroxide in the gel.
  • The hydrogen peroxide will then penetrate the porous surface of your tooth enamel to reach deep stains and remove discoloration.
  • After this process is over, you’ll find that your teeth are now several shades whiter.

While you may experience some sensitivity during the treatment, the warm light should alleviate any of your discomfort. We can also provide anti-sensitivity toothpaste before your treatment if you have sensitive gums. Follow-up care for this treatment requires occasional touch-ups with Zoom! Weekender or Nite White gel along with regular brushing and flossing.
