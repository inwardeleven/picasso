Pediatric Dentistry

An important decisions you'll have to make for your child is choosing which dentist to go to. You want to choose one that understands your child's needs and also has the experience to provide the right dental treatments and care. As your premier provider of pediatric dentistry in and around El Paso, TX, we at Picasso Smiles Dental hope to make that decision simple for you. With our compassionate care and quality dental treatments, we strive to be your choice when you're in search of an El Paso pediatric dentist.


Visiting Your El Paso Pediatric Dentist

When you visit our El Paso office, you'll be greeted by our warm and friendly staff in our comfortable and relaxing environment. One of our kids' dentists will provide a thorough dental examination to determine the care that your child needs. Some of the different dental treatments that we offer for children include:

  • Fluoride Treatment
  • Dental Sealants
  • Dental Sedation (For extensive cases and anxious patients)

In addition, your child's visit with our El Paso dentists will consist of education for you and your little one on the best oral hygiene methods to maintain healthy dental habits.


Contact The Choice In El Paso Pediatric Dentistry

If you're looking to schedule an appointment with your local El Paso pediatric dentist, we encourage you to give us a call at (915) 587-6453 for our Westside office or (915) 857-6453 for our Eastside office. Alternatively, you can fill out our online appointment request form to schedule your appointment with us. Please don't hesitate to let us know if you have any questions. We look forward to hearing from you!
